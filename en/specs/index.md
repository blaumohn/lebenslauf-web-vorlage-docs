The specs build the public project map.
They explain the project as a connected system of product, docs,
working framework, and recurring flows.

This layer serves as a shared entry point for organisational, product, and
technical readers. It bundles purpose, system boundaries, relationships, and
stable flows in one consistent format.

## Reading logic

- **Project spec**:
  entry point into the overall system.
- **System specs**:
  stable definitions of main systems and subsystems.
- **Flow specs**:
  stable handovers between systems.

These pages intentionally do not form a rigid tree.
They work with:

- parent systems
- child systems
- related systems
- related flows
