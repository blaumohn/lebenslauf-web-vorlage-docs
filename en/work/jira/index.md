The public Jira area shows the public work state without Jira Cloud links.
This entry point deliberately separates current work, history, and the active
sprint view.

- [Sprint Board]({{ "/en/jira/sprint/" | relative_url }})
- [Backlog]({{ "/en/jira/backlog/" | relative_url }})
- [History]({{ "/en/jira/history/" | relative_url }})
- [Sprint History]({{ "/en/work/sprints/" | relative_url }})

## Orientation

- **Backlog** shows active or deliberately deferred work.
- **History** bundles sprint history and globally visible completed top-level
  issues.
- **Sprint Board** shows only the active sprint slice.
